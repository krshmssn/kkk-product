Obscura Detection System

Innovative Technology for Search and Recovery

Our human remains detection technology, Obscura, is a handheld thermal-based system that identifies possible locations of human remains using thermal imaging and infrared light. The system uses a smartphone connected to a thermal camera and an IR flashlight, enabling field operatives to detect subtle temperature differences that may indicate buried or concealed human remains. Designed to support search-and-recovery operations, Obscura offers a compact, affordable, and accessible alternative to conventional methods.

This tool can aid forensic teams, disaster responders, and humanitarian missions by enhancing visibility in critical situations.

Smartphone-Connected System

Using an Android smartphone as the central processing device allows for a familiar interface and leverages existing mobile technology to reduce cost and improve accessibility.

Thermal Camera

The compact USB-C thermal camera provides accurate surface temperature detection. It's small, lightweight, and compatible with most Android devices, making it ideal for fieldwork.

Infrared Flashlight

An external IR flashlight boosts the visibility of heat signatures in dark environments or shaded areas. It enhances the detection range and accuracy, especially during nighttime operations.
